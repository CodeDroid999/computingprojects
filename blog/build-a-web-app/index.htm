What Type of Web App They Can Consider Developing in 2022?

While there are millions of applications available on the app store, choosing the best app development idea that can assure your business success can be a challenging task. So before you drill straight into the factors affecting the app development cost, let’s have a look at the types of applications that you can build in 2022…

Different Types of Web Applications You Can Build in 2022

  • Multi-Page Apps (MPA)

This type of web app means, having a web app that contains multiple pages and offers different features of an MPA on different pages. This means when a user clicks on a button or link, a request is initiated to load a new page. Multi-page applications are crafted in a way that suits the modern needs of the marketplaces, elearning sites and other platforms. So no matter which industry domain you are planning to enter, MPA is the default choice if you want to build a web app. 

  • Single Page Applications (SPA)

In recent times, SPAs are catching the hype in the business world. Since a single page application is quick to navigate, all the functionalities are easily accessible from one page. Clicking a button doesn’t have to refresh the page and works flawlessly even on the stored cache or in offline mode. As SPAs are quick to load and ensure an excellent user experience, therefore, helping businesses to drive heavy traffic to the website.

  • Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)

Don’t get confused between the terms progressive web apps and web apps, both the terms are used simultaneously. But unfortunately, they are not the same. All the PAWs can be recalled as web applications but all web apps are not PWAs. Progressive web apps include the characteristics of both web and native apps. PWAs work as web apps but also boast the features of native apps such as offline accessibility, access to device hardware and push notifications. 

As you noticed that different types of web apps offer different features and suit different goals, therefore, before you proceed, it is worth hiring a web app development company that suggests you an appropriate web app development option. How Much Does It Cost To Build a Web App in 2022?

It is always challenging to answer the question that how much does it cost to build a web app in 2022? Even the most experienced app development company fails to provide an estimation without knowing the nature of your app. In fact, businesses have different requirements, therefore different apps are developed for different purposes. Hence, it is hard to provide a precise estimate of the cost without knowing the actual requirements of the business.

The app development cost is majorly dependent upon various factors including features, complexity, UI/UX design, app testing and more.

In fact, estimating the app development is like buying a car. Like it has various car models, colour options, designs and more to evaluate, web app development also involves various factors while estimating the app.

Below is the breakdown of different factors and considerations that you can ignore to evaluate when estimating the app development cost.

  • Understanding the App Types

There are many apps like Amazon, eBay, Talabat, Doordash, Netflix, Instagram and more in different domains that encourage startups with their success stories. But before you get excited and take a step ahead directly into the app development process, you need to evaluate whether it fits your budget and business needs?     

These are the industry giants that won’t happen overnight. If you are choosing to build such huge apps, then make sure you have enough time and budget to invest. 

But before you pour your hard efforts into replicating the same app, it is worth identifying the scope of the app. Take the time to evaluate your business needs and understand what type of app you will be required for your business. Some of the popular app types include e-commerce apps, social networking apps, on-demand apps, data-driven apps, and even functionality apps. Each app type includes a different set of features. So to avoid any confusion, it is worth including market research and determining the type of app you will be required for your business.

  • Choice of operating Platform

Where you are planning to market your app is also affecting the app development budget. On the broadside, there are two options: either create a Native app or choose a hybrid app development. Wondering how it is affecting the app development cost? Before you get straight into the process of hiring a mobile app development company, you need to get into the details to understand how it is impacting the overall budget.

Let’s dig deep in…

You can choose to create a native mobile app when you are planning to create a platform-specific app for Android and iOS.

The biggest thing to consider is that the native framework uses programming languages and tools that are native to a particular platform. You need to use platform-specific tools and programming languages to make your app run on specific native platforms. To develop two native apps, you need to hire app development experts in different languages including Java, Objective-C, Kotlin, Swift and more. 

Since native apps are built for a specific platform, therefore they ensure excellent programs and services within the devices and offer optimum performance. But the downside is, that if you need to create native apps for different platforms, then you need to create a platform-specific codebase. You can hire an app developer to start writing the codebase, but it will take time to create codes. The increasing number of development hours means the higher the app development budget will be. 

Hybrid apps on the other hand are the economical option as they use the same codebase across the different platforms. This actually makes them compatible with several devices. With the cross-platform app development framework, you will need to build a single app that seamlessly releases on multiple platforms and is able to run on different devices using the same codebase.     

Hybrid apps have also received flak performance issues, and app crashes, which is why the majority of developers recommend the native approach. But if it comes to saving on budget, then it is worth looking for a hybrid approach. 

  • Location of the Developer

The cost to create a web app is majorly dependent upon the location you choose to hire an app development company. The hourly cost of the app developers is majorly dependent upon their skills, experience, knowledge and more. The average cost per hour for app development in North America can reach up to $100 to $150, whereas it can be as low as $20 to $30 in India.

Before you make any decision, keep in mind that hiring app developers at the expensive hourly cost will not give you any assurance of excellent quality whereas the lowest cost won’t mean that developers are lacking in skills. India is boasting a huge market for app developers therefore due to cut-throat competition, India is recognized as the cheapest place to hire the best app developers.

Rest the hourly cost of the app developer is also affected by the project requirements.

  • Complexity of the App

App development costs also rise with the complexity of the app. Different apps have different complexity levels and it is measured by their functionality. If you are wondering how will you determine the complexity of the app, then here we have categorized the complexity into three broad categories:

  • Basic Apps: These are the basic app structures that do not require data servers. Basic app versions are more like an MVP solution that involves basic functionalities. Prime examples of basic app types are Calendar apps, calculators, weather forecasting apps and more. The average cost to create a basic app can float around $10,000 to $12,000 per platform.
  • Medium Apps: These are the types of apps that require remote servers and require more sophisticated programming. The development costs for such apps range between $20,000 to $25,000.
  • Advanced Mobile App Version: It is an advanced version of the mobile app that boasts complex functionality. It includes the features and functionalities that fit the modern market needs. 
  • App Features and Functionalities

There is no doubt in this fact that features and functionalities are the major success-driven factors of web apps. But the number of features and their complexity also impact the final app development cost. Therefore, instead of stuffing your app with an end number of features and various elements, it is worth prioritizing the unique features that add a competitive edge to your business without putting much pressure on the budget.     

These are the major factors that you need to evaluate while estimating the app development cost of web apps. While these are the estimations, the final pricing of the app can go up and down depending upon the requirements of your business app. So before you make any decision, it is always recommended to book a free consultation slot with experts.

Conclusion

With the fact that having a digital presence of your business has become necessary, organizations from all across the world are looking for web app development solutions. But the moment you decide to create a business app, your primary concern would be how much does it cost you to build a web app in 2022? The simple way is to hire a web app development company and discuss your business requirements to get real project estimations. Leveraging the domain expertise and industry best knowledge, app developers will ensure you excellent app development solutions that add an edge to your business. All you need is to set the right budget by keeping in mind these important parameters.
