Steps Followed By The Web Developer To Build a Dynamic Website

A professional website is a reflection of a brand and how the business of that brand would be. A website is undoubtedly the first impression on the website visitors about the brand and just everything else pertaining to the business. A website should be perfectly designed and developed by the expert web developer who has had past experience of creating such websites. If you don’t choose an expert website developer with a lot of previous experience in creating the website in your niche, there are chances that the website built will not be perfect at all.

Don’t you want a great website to represent your business or brand?

Would you compromise with the quality of the website built by an unprofessional web development company? Well, your answers to the above questions should be very much wisely thought of as a small ignorance or compromise can lead to a bad future of the website. The key difference in the websites created by a web developer who is expert and an inexperienced developer is that when you hire web developer with a significant amount of experience in creating some really creative and unique websites, he knows the steps as well as the phases that are involved in developing and prior the development process- how designing the website with wholesome heart is important as it is the fundamental basis of the website.

 

We literally can’t say that development is more important, because of the coding part seems to be tough to everyone! Nope, it isn’t the case at all. Designing a website is as important as giving attention to every minute detail in the development process by any web development company as they are known by the professionalism and expertise that they put into creating some really robust and dynamic websites for their clients at all levels.

 

Phases of designing and developing a dynamic website are as following

As we already mentioned above that designing and development phases of a website are as important as both the sides of a coin but it is the designing part that comes first than the development (Or the coding stuff) part.

So, in this article, we will discuss about both the sides of the coin in an organised manner due to the fact that we are emphasizing how the phases of developing a website is important compared to some irregular or disorganised way of developing a website. We are not discussing the things differentiated on the basis of web development or designing, these are discussed in step by step manner as how a web developer who is expert and well-versed in his field takes forward the things to give a leap to the client website and make it a successful one.

  • 1. Idea conception for client website
  • 2. Planning after brainstorming
  • 3. Designing the wireframe
  • 4. Coding or developing according to the wireframe
  • 5. Submission after rigorous quality checks
  • 6. Maintenance of the client website

The steps we have mentioned above are the steps that most of the top-tier companies in web design and development or when you hire web developer they follow to give a competitive edge to the websites compared to others.

 

Let’s now discuss these steps in detail

Idea conception for client website

The first step in designing a successful website is to gather information. There are some things which you should consider not about the website’s appearance but the purpose of the whole existence of the website that is going to be built. The first step is actually the most important step because it requires a profound understanding of its creative enterprise. It contains a good understanding of what your goals and dreams are and how to use the website to achieve these goals. A web developer is important to help them understand the needs of your business or website by asking many questions.

The things to look before you leap are the following ones

Purpose of the website

What is the aim of a website? Does the client wants to provide information, promote services, or sell products?

Long-term Goal of the website

What do you want to do by building this site? Two more common goals are to earn money and share information with your target audience.

Focus on the Target audience

Are there any people who will help you achieve your goal? It would be helpful to describe the “ideal” character you want to use on your website. Looking at the age, gender, or benefit of your prospective customers and website users – this will help you choose the best design for your site.

Information and Content to go on website

What information does the target audience look for on your website? Are you looking for specific information, products, or services online?

Planning after brainstorming

Information gathered from the first step is used now to plan your website. This is where the website sitemap is created. The Sitemap is a list of all the main topics in the website and the sub-header (if any). Since the development of a coherent, easy-to-understand navigation system is indispensable, this guide can be used as a web content. In designing your own customer site, the site must be mindful of the end user. After all, these people know about the service or buy your product. You must create an excellent user-interface so as to give the best user-experience to the users of the website, it’s easy to navigate through a website, this is the fundamental basis of any kind of website and every web development company should know about these facts for sure.

During the design phase, the web designer will also help evaluate the technology that is to be implemented in the website later on. Without designing the site, it is wise to include elements and discussing all issues such as WordPress and other Content Management System (CMS)

Designing the wireframe

According to the information gathered so far, the appearance and feel of the website is to be decided on time. Target customers are one of the most important factors to be considered. For example, the Teens website is very different from the financial institution’s website and so on. It is also important to strengthen the company’s identity on the website as part of the company logo and the color element phase elements. A web designer prototypes the website. This is usually the final design to display a .jpg image. Other designers may also use the website’s secure area to allow customers to see the work being done under normal circumstances, the e-mail address for the website template.

Coding or developing according to the wireframe

In the development work, a website is coded or created. At this point, when you hire web developer, he is responsible to get prototypes from all the individual graphical elements and uses them to create the actual activity of the site. This is usually done by developing a home page and supplementing the internal page “shell” first. As it contains the site’s most important navigation structure, the shell is used as the content of the page template page.

Once you’ve created a structure, the website design gets the content and distributes it across the entire site. Elements such as words (Content Management System) such as WordPress as well as online shopping cart are implemented and the functionality is implemented at this stage.

Submission after rigorous quality checks

At this point, the web designer as well as the developer along with the Quality Analyst focus on the final details and tests the site. They check the features of all the other scripts (see differences in different web browsers) such as the latest browsers, such as forms, the contents of the previous test. Compatibility issues and tests are properly verified.

A web designer who runs the current website design and development standards. The basic techniques used are HTML and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets). The current code represents a website based on current development standards- When it comes to cross-connectivity and other useful audit issues.

Maintenance of the client website

While most of the web development companies may say goodbye to their clients after they have submitted the website after designing and development or testing, we would suggest you taking the help and support of the web development company which can promise you post-submission services and support for the maintenance of the websites. We believe that bad times never come after knocking the door, it can come to anyone and everyone, anytime and everywhere!

Conclusion

A professional company would make sure to be your helping hand in times of need so that you are not left alone when the website crashes or some other issues may arise regarding the server and you may not be technically efficient to take the things further yourself. Be clear about this fact and service before you hire web developer after careful consideration of the services that the company or the freelancers would be able to give you.
