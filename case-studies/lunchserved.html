About Lunchserved

LunchServed is an innovative app that focuses on providing the most convenient dining experience. It restores the precious time by eliminating the entire wait associated with dining at a restaurant. Using the app, Customers can book the table, order their meal and arrive at the restaurant to be seated and served with the payment already processed. It help users so they can spend less time in waiting and enjoy their food. LunchServed has the ability to invite friends, family, co-workers, or anyone else to join for the meal. They can add their meal to the order and the customers can split the payment right in the app. LunchServed is also supporting the corporates whereby the companies and businesses can provide their employees with company paid lunch and dinner. Also, there is a separate restaurant app allowing the merchants to manage their profile and business generated through the platform with ease.

Key Challenges

  • System provides with a real time update of the orders and information for the businesses as well as for the customers.
  • Real time interaction amongst the Restaurant Owners and Users using separate Restaurant App and User app.
  • Sharing of bill amongst the users who are having lunch/dinner together.
  • Social Booking through the apps so that the customers can book their order individually.
  • Business lunch feature is incorporated allowing the company to create business accounts and their employees to create their individual account. The company can select the employees and add to their accounts, employees can go for lunch and pay from their account through the money which was allocated to them by their employers.

    • Proposition & Solution

    • Designed and developed Android and iOS mobile apps and a representative landing page website for LunchServed. Different app is provided for Restaurants and Customers using the latest cutting edge tools and technologies.
    • Developed Social Booking in the apps so that the customers can book the same order and pay for it individually.
    • Developed Business Lunch in the apps by which the company can create their business account, select the employees and pay for the employees which go for lunch or dinner in the restaurant.

    Results & Benefits

    The solution designed and developed by Xicom met all of the requirements of LunchServed owner and his vision and it quickly gained a userbase of 10,000 registered users within a short period of less than 3 months.

    • LunchServed was delivered the mobile apps and website on time thus allowing them with appropriate time to market their product.
    • LunchServed is extremely satisfied with the outcome of this project, and the quality of the mobile apps and the website delivered to them as they met all of their expectations.
    • New features introduced within the system were taken up and Xicom ensured the faster delivery for the same to take the system to the next level. This overall dedicated effort from Xicom along with the overall software quality procedures enhanced client satisfaction, thereby increasing customer retention.
    • The Android and iOS mobile applications developed by Xicom has been appreciated a lot from all kinds of users i.e. customers, restaurant owners and business owners and they have gained very high reviews from the users, further strengthening LunchServed relationship with its customers.
    • LunchServed worked with Xicom by hiring dedicated resources (DTM Model) and fully utilized the flexibility to bring change in scope so as to get the final product as per their requirement and meeting all the standards of their respective business domain.
